Spirited Away

Introduction Table Video

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

Genre: Fantasy / Coming-of-age

Year Released: 2001

Awards: Academy Award (Best Animated Feature)
Fun Fact: The bathhouse world is packed with folklore-inspired details.

Spirited Away follows Chihiro, a girl who stumbles into a mysterious spirit world and must work in a bathhouse to survive.
Along the way, she learns courage, identity, and empathy even when everything feels impossible.
One of my favorite themes is how small choices can lead to big changes, especially when you hold on to your kindness.
